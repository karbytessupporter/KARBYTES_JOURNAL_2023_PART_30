The point of this journal entry is to move on from the previous journal entry because I feel that I am far away from where I want to be mentally. It may take a while to get closer to where I want to be mentally. What seems to be preventing me from maintaining a frame of mind which I think is adequately conducive to my goals and values is the intrusive interference of brainwashing propaganda from literal thought police and perhaps even from civilians who take the side of my brainwashers. I seem to be outnumbered and cornered into being brainwashed and sabotaged against my will. That leads me to my next and most important point in this journal entry thus far…

I think that I really should, as soon as possible, “cold turkey” (instead of tapering off gradually) quit my “addiction” to disclosing the intimate details of my moment-to-moment experiences online because I think that such comprehensive disclosure makes it hard for me to attain an adequate “buffer space” between the character I portray myself to be online and the living, breathing information processing agent I am which is writing and reading the content is used to substantiate that character. In other words, I think that taking a more emotionally distant and depersonalized stance to managing my public identity online (and “in person”) will help me to not feel so suffocated, engulfed, and oppressed as a result of being so intensely “on display” while constantly giving into the impulse to explain myself to real or imaginary (human) audiences. Hence, this might as well be the last time I ever document my personal experiences. It could be that, after this blog post is published and backed up that all of my new content will be about subject matter which excludes disclosures about how I am feeling (but I will disclose pragmatic details about my life as minimally as possible for what is needed to manage my personal brand and to fulfill my desires such as news about major changes to my health status and socioeconomic status as though I were some anonymous writer of a Wikipedia article written about karbytes (or Karlina Ray Beringer) the public figure).

There. I might as well be dead to some. What I add to my personal websites and social media profiles will likely only be media items pertaining to dry textbook-like computer programming tutorials, academic philosophy articles, and news items I find which pertain to my interests within the scope of physics, mathematics, computer science, and philosophy. (I intend to “waste” no more time explaining to the public what my preferred gender identity is, musical tastes are, sexual orientation is, dietary preferences are, career preferences are, and any other personal details which seem inappropriately intimate in comparison to the new and improved brand I am trying to promote which is essentially devoid of personal disclosure in favor of being strictly limited to impersonal subject matter presented in a way which lacks a sense of personality. If karbytes is remembered for anything, it will (hopefully) be some eccentric, delinquent, and perhaps troubled human who obsessively documented itself via personal journal writing for no more than three years and then, suddenly, “went dark” in June 2023 to apparently be replaced by some kind of robot).

Now, just because the content which I publish online in the future is expected to be devoid of personality and have the feel of an extremely logical and emotionless robot does not mean that I won’t continue making music and other forms of art which may be very emotional. I just intend to do it as a relatively egoless robot; a mere information processing agent immersed in hedonistic flow within the context of a resource management game.
