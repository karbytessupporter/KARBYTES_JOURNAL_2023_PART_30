Tonight after smoking a modest amount of dried home-grown cannabis (and not very high quality cannabis but, nevertheless, of decent quality for inducing a functional high) I got the following ideas which seemed useful to write down:

It is beyond the scope of my abilities as an ego to determine whether or not I am being phony. Someone other than myself would presumably be able to discern my degree of phoniness better than I ever could because I structurally lack a perceptive and cognitive distance between myself (as object) and myself (as observer).

Perhaps the previous claims are examples of phoniness. What might be truer is the claim that it is beyond the scope of my abilities as an ego to determine whether or not I have attained omniscience.
